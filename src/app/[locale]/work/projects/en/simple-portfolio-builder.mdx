## Problem Overview

The increasing reliance on email has led to inbox clutter caused by promotional emails and subscriptions. Users face challenges in efficiently managing their inboxes, which negatively impacts productivity and focus. Outlook users, particularly professionals, value organization and cleanliness, emphasizing the need for a streamlined solution.

## Proposed Solution: Subscription Manager

##### Key Features
- **Centralized Management**: View and manage subscriptions from a central hub
- **Mass Unsubscribe**: Quickly remove unwanted subscriptions
- **Custom Alerts**: Set preferences (e.g., daily or weekly updates)
- **Unsubscribe Suggestions**: Notifications for unopened subscriptions
- **Undo Functionality**: Recover mistakenly unsubscribed items

##### Future Enhancements
- **Analytics**: Insights into subscription habits and productivity impact
- **Machine Learning**: Prioritize high-value emails based on behavior and context

## Audience & Goals

##### Primary Audience
- Professionals and academics using Outlook

##### Goals
- Increase productivity by minimizing inbox clutter
- Encourage inbox organization habits
- Improve the perception of Outlook as a professional tool

##### Success Metrics
- Usage frequency
- Increase in unsubscriptions
- Task completion times
- Net promoter scores

## Development Timeline
1. **Pilot (2 months)**: Internal testing
2. **Beta (3–4 months)**: Gradual user rollout
3. **Launch (6 months)**: Feature availability for all users

## Value Proposition
The Outlook Subscription Manager saves time, enhances focus, and promotes an organized inbox, aligning with Microsoft’s mission to empower productivity.
